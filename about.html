About Devona Accounting

Practical support, built on careful work

Tom Devona helps individuals, freelancers, and small business owners stay organized with tax preparation support, bookkeeping, and clear financial recordkeeping. The goal is simple: make the process easier to understand, easier to manage, and less stressful when deadlines approach.

Our approach

Clear help for real financial tasks

Many people do not need complicated accounting language. They need someone who can help sort through documents, organize transactions, prepare for filing, and explain what information matters. Devona Accounting focuses on practical support for everyday taxpayers and small business owners.

  • Help sorting through documents
  • Organizing transactions and records
  • Preparing for filing season
  • Explaining what information matters
  • Practical support for everyday needs
Local and remote clients

Serving locally and remotely

Devona Accounting proudly serves local clients in Walker, Madison, Montgomery, and surrounding Texas counties, and remote clients nationwide. Whether you are preparing an individual return, catching up on bookkeeping, or organizing small business records, the process is designed to be clear and manageable.

What you can expect

Professional, detail-oriented support

Tax and bookkeeping work requires accuracy, organization, and trust. Devona Accounting is built around careful record review, clear communication, and helping clients understand the next step before work begins.
